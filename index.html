The Methodology: How?

This framework turns long documents into action-ready communication for faster decisions.

Logic Clusters

Organize thoughts using MECE to eliminate overlaps and logical gaps before writing.

Outcome: cleaner sections with fewer reviewer comments.

Inverted Stacks

Lead with the conclusion. Respect reader time by ranking findings by impact.

Outcome: leadership understands the ask in under one minute.

Visual GPS

Engineer for scannability. Use white space and signposting as visual roadmaps.

Outcome: faster scanning without missing critical points.

No Overlaps

Arguments stand alone. Repetition indicates broken logic.

Each section must add a new decision-relevant point.

No Gaps

The 'So What?' Test

"It is important to note that we are currently experiencing a decline in market share."
"Our market share is declining."

If a sentence hides the decision, rewrite it until action is obvious.

Critical Pitfalls

Avoid these patterns to protect clarity and decision speed.

Burying the Lead

Findings at the end force reader struggle. Dropout is immediate.

Passive Stance

"Mistakes were made." Hides ownership and creates ambiguity.
